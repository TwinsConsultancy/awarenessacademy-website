Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At AWARENESS ACADEMY, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

Key Points: We never sell your personal data to third parties. We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our services. You have control over your data and can request its deletion at any time.

2. Information Collected Automatically

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide access to courses and content
  • Track your learning progress
  • Issue certificates of completion
  • Process payments and transactions

Communication

  • Send course updates and announcements
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send promotional emails (with your consent)
  • Notify you of important changes to our services

Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and courses
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Fix technical issues and bugs

Security & Compliance

  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality (login, security)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect data about platform usage

Managing Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

Information Sharing & Disclosure

Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information with:

Service Providers:

  • Payment processors (for transaction handling)
  • Email service providers (for communications)
  • Cloud hosting services (for data storage)
  • Analytics providers (for platform improvement)

Course Instructors:

  • Student progress and performance metrics

  • Compliance with laws and regulations
  • Response to legal requests and court orders
  • Protection of our rights and property
  • Prevention of fraud or illegal activities

Business Transfers:

  • In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Users will be notified of any ownership changes

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted databases and secure servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • Password Security: Hashed and salted password storage
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and alerts
Note: While we use reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access & Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Export your course progress and certificates
  • View your account activity history

Correction & Update

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Modify your communication preferences

Deletion & Erasure

  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Remove specific information from your profile
  • Note: Some data may be retained for legal/business purposes

Opt-Out Rights

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Disable non-essential cookies
  • Opt out of data analytics (where applicable)

To exercise your rights: Contact us at privacy@awarenessacademy.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 18, you must have parental or guardian consent to use our services.

If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information can contact us at privacy@awarenessacademy.com.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that:

  • Data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws
  • Adequate protection measures are in place
  • Third parties adhere to privacy standards

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Retention Periods:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Course Completion Data: Retained permanently (for certificates)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Continued use after changes means acceptance
