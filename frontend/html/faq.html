Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our spiritual courses, enrollment process, and programs at Awareness Academy.

General Questions

What is Awareness Academy?

Awareness Academy is a premier spiritual development center founded in 2014 by Abhishek Rajan. Located in Thammampatty, Salem District, Tamil Nadu, we offer transformative courses in meditation, Reiki healing, yoga, mudra therapy, acupuncture, and personal development programs. Our mission is to help individuals achieve spiritual growth, inner peace, and holistic well-being.

Where is Awareness Academy located?

Awareness Academy is located in Thammampatty, Salem District, Tamil Nadu, India. We are easily accessible from Salem city and well-connected by road. Contact us at +91 98433 41742 for directions or to schedule a visit.

Who founded Awareness Academy?

Awareness Academy was founded in 2014 by Abhishek Rajan, a dedicated spiritual guide with over a decade of experience in meditation, holistic healing, and personal transformation. He has trained thousands of students in spiritual practices and continues to lead the academy with passion and expertise.

What languages are courses taught in?

Our courses are primarily taught in English and Tamil. Hindi instruction is also available for certain programs. Online courses have English subtitles and materials to ensure accessibility for learners from various regions.

Course Information

What courses does Awareness Academy offer?

We offer comprehensive courses in Meditation Training, Reiki Healing Certification, Yoga Teacher Training, Mudra Therapy, Acupuncture, Personal Development, Youth Leadership Programs, and Self-Reliant Lifestyle Coaching. Both online and offline programs are available to suit your learning preferences.

Are online courses available?

Yes, we offer both online and in-person courses. Our online programs include high-quality video lessons, live interactive sessions, downloadable course materials, and certification upon completion. You can learn from anywhere in India or abroad at your own pace.

Do I need prior experience to join courses?

No prior experience is required for our beginner-level courses. We welcome absolute beginners and guide you step-by-step through the fundamentals. Advanced courses may have prerequisites which are clearly mentioned in the course descriptions.

How long are the certification programs?

Course duration varies based on the program. Basic courses range from 2-4 weeks, intermediate programs are 1-3 months, and comprehensive certification programs can be 3-6 months. Each course page has detailed duration information and curriculum details.

Will I receive a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, all our certification courses include an official certificate upon successful completion of the curriculum and assessments. Our certificates are professionally designed and enhance your credentials as a practitioner or teacher in your chosen field.
